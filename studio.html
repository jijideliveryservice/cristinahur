CRISTINA HUR

Inspired by the simple things in life, I create high-quality, holistic brand expressions rooted in aesthetic clarity. An emotional, tactile boutique branding studio born along the shores of the Pacific, weaving stories across cultures and continents.

Shaped by a global perspective and an appreciation for cultural nuance, my work is rooted in simplicity, restraint, and quiet refinement. Drawing from experience across design, strategy, and visual storytelling, I’m drawn to work that feels personal and layered—projects that evolve over time, hold meaning, and reflect the people behind them. Whether through design, messaging, or thoughtful development, each collaboration is guided by intention, curiosity, and a shared commitment to building something lasting.

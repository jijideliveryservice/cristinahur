My name is Cristina, founder of a boutique branding studio based in Laguna Hills, California. I craft emotional, tactile brand experiences for clients across creative, real estate, and engineering industries.

Great design goes beyond aesthetics—it’s about building meaningful connections, telling authentic stories, and helping brands make a lasting impression.

Through collaboration and attention to detail, every project is tailored to reflect the unique vision and values of each client.

Born and raised in California and a traveller at heart–I concieved my studio whilst travelling in Japan with the global perspective and deep appreciation for cultural nuance inform a design philosophy rooted in simplicity, intentionality, and quiet refinement.
